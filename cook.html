As a cook at Fradel and Spies, you play a crucial role in delivering the exceptional culinary experience that our guests expect. You will have the opportunity to showcase your culinary skills and creativity in preparing high-quality dishes. Working in a fast-paced and dynamic kitchen environment, you will collaborate with fellow cooks and contribute to the overall success of our restaurant.
You would be a great cook if you:
  • Punctuality and Attendance: Demonstrate regular and consistent attendance, adhering to the assigned schedule, and arriving on time.

  • Available to work flexible hours that may include early mornings, evenings, weekends, nights and/or holidays

  • Adherence to Standards: Maintain the restaurant's operating policies and standards, including food quality, portion control, hygiene, and safety practices

  • Culinary Expertise: Prepare dishes according to standard recipes or customise them to meet customer preferences, including adjusting cooking times, ingredient quantities, or substitutions

  • Attention to Detail: Pay close attention to food presentation, ensuring that each dish meets our aesthetic standards and is visually appealing to guests

  • Team Collaboration: Work effectively as part of a culinary team, communicating and coordinating with other cooks, chefs, and kitchen staff to ensure smooth operations and timely delivery of food orders.

  • Adaptability: Adapt to different responsibilities within the kitchen, such as prep work, line cooking, plating, or inventory management, as required during each shift

  • Cleanliness and Organization: Maintain a clean and organized work area, following proper sanitation and food handling protocols to ensure a safe and hygienic kitchen environment.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  • Culinary Skills: Possess a strong foundation in culinary techniques, including knife skills, cooking methods, and flavour combinations.

  • Quick Learning: Have the ability to quickly learn new recipes, cooking techniques, and kitchen procedures to enhance your culinary repertoire.

  • Adaptability: Be open to learning and adapting to changes in the menu, ingredients, or cooking processes, ensuring flexibility in meeting guest demands.

  • Time Management: Demonstrate efficient time management skills, effectively multitasking and prioritizing tasks to meet food preparation and service deadlines.

  • Collaboration: Work collaboratively with the kitchen team, displaying effective communication, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.
