As a server at Fradel and Spies, you play a pivotal role in delivering the exceptional dining experience that our guests expect. You will have the opportunity to showcase your hospitality skills and provide attentive service to create a memorable dining experience. Working in a fast-paced and dynamic environment, you will collaborate with colleagues and contribute to the overall success of our restaurant.
You would be a great server if you:
  • Punctuality and Attendance: Demonstrate regular and consistent attendance, adhering to the assigned schedule, and arriving on time.

  • Available to work flexible hours that may include early mornings, evenings, weekends, nights and/or holidays

  • Adherence to Standards: Maintain the restaurant's service policies and standards, including delivering exceptional customer service, accurately taking orders, handling payments, and following service protocols.

  • Customer Engagement: Engage with guests, providing friendly and personalised service, anticipating their needs, and offering recommendations to enhance their dining experience.

  • Menu Knowledge: Familiarise yourself with our menu offerings, including ingredients, preparation methods, and specials, to confidently answer guest inquiries and make recommendations.

  • Team Collaboration: Work effectively as part of a service team, communicating and coordinating with other servers, hosts, kitchen staff, and management to ensure smooth operations and guest satisfaction.

  • Attention to Detail: Pay attention to detail when taking orders, ensuring accuracy and special requests are properly communicated to the kitchen staff for timely and precise execution.

  • Adaptability: Adapt to various tasks and responsibilities within the restaurant, such as assisting with bussing tables, setting up dining areas, or supporting other service areas, as needed during each shift.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  • Strong Communication: Possess excellent verbal communication skills, actively listening to guest requests, and effectively conveying information to guests and colleagues.

  • Multitasking: Demonstrate the ability to handle multiple tables and tasks simultaneously while maintaining a high level of service and attention to detail

  • Problem-Solving: Exhibit strong problem-solving skills, resolving guest concerns or complaints promptly and professionally, seeking assistance when necessary.

  • Salesmanship: Utilise suggestive selling techniques to upsell menu items, beverages, and specials, increasing guest satisfaction and restaurant revenue.

  • Positive Attitude: Maintain a positive and enthusiastic demeanour, creating a welcoming and enjoyable atmosphere for guests and colleagues alike.
