As a restaurant host at Fradel and Spies, your role is crucial in creating the exceptional "Fradel and Spies" dining experience for our guests. You will have the opportunity to showcase your hospitality skills and ensure that every guest feels welcome and attended to. As a restaurant host at Fradel and Spies, your role is vital in delivering the exceptional dining experience we're known for. You'll welcome and attend to guests, manage seating arrangements, and collaborate with the team.
You would be a great host/hostess if you:
  • Punctuality and Attendance: Demonstrate regular and consistent attendance, adhering to the assigned schedule, and arriving on time.

  • Available to work flexible hours that may include early mornings, evenings, weekends, nights and/or holidays

  • Compliance with Standards: Uphold the restaurant's operating policies and standards, including maintaining service quality, handling cash transactions, ensuring store safety and security, and following protocols with or without reasonable accommodation

  • Customer Engagement: Engage with our customers, establishing a rapport and understanding their needs through clear and pleasant communication. Provide prompt and courteous assistance to enhance their dining experience

  • Seating Management: Efficiently manage the seating arrangements, ensuring optimal utilisation of the restaurant's capacity while considering guest preferences and any special requests.

  • Team Collaboration: Work collaboratively as part of a team, effectively communicating with servers, kitchen staff, and other team members to facilitate smooth operations and guest satisfaction.

  • Relationship Building: Foster positive relationships with guests by demonstrating genuine care and attentiveness, creating a welcoming atmosphere that encourages their return.

  • Adaptability: Adapt to varying responsibilities within the restaurant, such as assisting with taking orders, coordinating take-out orders, or answering phone calls, to ensure efficient service during each shift.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  • Quick Learning: Possess the ability to quickly grasp new information, such as menu items, daily specials, and restaurant procedures, and apply it effectively in your role.

  • Instruction Comprehension: Understand and carry out both oral and written instructions accurately. Seek clarification when needed to ensure tasks are performed correctly.

  • Interpersonal Skills: Display strong interpersonal skills, including effective communication, active listening, and empathy, to create positive interactions with guests and colleagues.

  • Teamwork: Work collaboratively with your team members, promoting a harmonious and cooperative work environment.

  • Relationship Building: Develop and nurture relationships with guests, establishing a loyal customer base for the restaurant.
