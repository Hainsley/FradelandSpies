As a Fradel and Spies barista, you will create that "Fradel and Spies" experience for our guests through excellent service and expertly-crafted products. You will be in this restaurant where you will have the ability to master your food & beverage craft, work alongside friends and meet new people everyday.
You would be a great barista if you:
  • Punctuality and Attendance: Demonstrate regular and consistent attendance, arriving on time for shifts, and maintaining a reliable presence.

  • Flexibility: Be available to work flexible hours, including early mornings, evenings, weekends, and holidays, to accommodate the varied preferences of our guests.

  • Adherence to Standards: Uphold Fradel and Spies' high-quality standards for beverage preparation, including consistency, taste, presentation, and adherence to recipes.

  • Customer Engagement: Engage with customers, greet them warmly, and take the time to understand their preferences, providing personalised recommendations and exceptional service.

  • Beverage Craftsmanship: Expertly prepare a variety of coffee and tea beverages, showcasing your knowledge of brewing methods, latte art, and flavour profiles to deliver an outstanding experience.

  • Speed and Efficiency: Work efficiently in a fast-paced environment, maintaining a focus on quality while ensuring timely delivery of orders to keep up with customer demand.

  • Cleanliness and Organization: Maintain a clean and organised work area, including the coffee station and equipment, ensuring a hygienic environment and smooth operations.

  • Team Collaboration: Collaborate effectively with other baristas, kitchen staff, and management, fostering teamwork and clear communication to ensure a seamless and enjoyable coffee service.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  • Communication Skills: Demonstrate excellent verbal communication skills, actively listening to customer preferences and effectively conveying information about beverages and specials.

  • Multitasking: Excel at managing multiple orders simultaneously, efficiently prioritising tasks, and ensuring accuracy and promptness in beverage preparation.

  • Adaptability: Be open to learning new recipes, techniques, and product offerings, staying updated on industry trends and incorporating them into your craft.

  • Passion for Service: Display a genuine passion for providing exceptional customer service, going above and beyond to ensure customer satisfaction and a positive experience./li>
