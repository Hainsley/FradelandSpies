DISHWASHER

As a server at Fradel and Spies, you play a pivotal role in delivering the exceptional dining experience that our guests expect. You will have the opportunity to showcase your hospitality skills and provide attentive service to create a memorable dining experience. Working in a fast-paced and dynamic environment, you will collaborate with colleagues and contribute to the overall success of our restaurant.
You would be a great dishwasher if you:
  • Punctuality and Attendance: Demonstrate regular and consistent attendance, adhering to the assigned schedule, and arriving on time.

  • Attention to Detail: Pay close attention to detail when handling dishes, ensuring thorough cleaning, sanitization, and proper storage.

  • Organization and Efficiency: Maintain an organised workspace, efficiently sorting and stacking dishes and utensils for effective cleaning and storage.

  • Adaptability: Adapt to varying demands, such as high volumes of dishes during peak hours, and effectively manage your time to keep up with kitchen operations.

  • Team Collaboration: Work well with the kitchen staff, communicating effectively and supporting a cooperative and productive work environment.

  • Cleanliness and Safety: Follow proper sanitation and safety protocols, including handling chemicals, maintaining clean work surfaces, and adhering to health and safety guidelines.

  • Equipment Maintenance: Report any malfunctioning equipment or maintenance needs promptly to ensure a smooth workflow and prevent disruptions.

  • Dependability: Demonstrate reliability in your role, completing assigned tasks efficiently and being available to assist as needed.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  • Physical Stamina: Possess the physical stamina to stand for long periods, lift heavy items, and work in a fast-paced environment.

  • Time Management: Efficiently manage your time, maintaining a steady flow of clean dishes to support kitchen operations and minimise delays.

  • Adaptability: Be open to learning and adapting to changes in procedures or equipment to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.

  • Attention to Safety: Prioritise safety measures, including using protective gear, following proper lifting techniques, and identifying and reporting any safety hazards.

  • Teamwork: Demonstrate the ability to work collaboratively with the kitchen staff, supporting each other to ensure smooth operations.
